QUALITY ASSURANCE

Eminence striving practice for better outcomes We keep excellence at the front of everything, whether interviewers, training, or the data. Let’s keep it modest, if you ensure Quality as your foundation the conclusion will indicate.



Ensuring Quality for CATI Interviews:

EW has devoted team members who are solely responsible for quality for all the surveys hence they are supervising interviews that are being conducted. We follow carefully created guidelines before performing any offline interviews.


We are tracking all completed interviews at 100% as follows.:

The voice quality team evaluates each and every finished interview call.

Audits are done on 80% of screening calls.

One of the Quality Analysts calls the respondent in 25% of all completed interviews to confirm their participation and get comments for the interviewer. T & C apply.

The call duration of each completed interview is verified in full. Any interview that does not meet the survey's LOI average is marked and examined to look for mistakes.

Ensuring Quality for Online Interviews:


For the purpose of adding respondents to our panel, the proper steps are done. To make sure you only receive the best survey findings, we focus more on quality once the data is gathered. We have a pre-formulated process which is followed to scan all interview data collected online.

Our quality assurance includes checks such as but are not limited to:

Trick questions are used to make sure that respondents are reading all questions attentively and providing thoughtful answers.

Speeders are used to make sure that responders are taking the time to read and comprehend the questions, rather than simply rushing through them.

Only those open-ended responses that make sense are included in the final responses once they have been examined. All useless or pointless info is removed.

IP addresses are inspected to make sure that no responder or organisation takes part in a survey more than once.

No matter if a study was developed by us or not, all studies pass a unique RID to ensure that no responder fills out the survey more than once.
